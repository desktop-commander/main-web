What this prompt does

Collect scattered invoices into one organized folder — AI searches your Downloads and Documents, identifies invoice files by name and content, and moves them to a dedicated accounting folder with a summary of what was found.

How it works

  1. 1.Run this prompt in Desktop Commander
  2. 2.AI searches your folders for invoice files and PDFs
  3. 3.Creates an organized accounting folder and moves invoices
  4. 4.Generates a summary report of all invoices found and organized

Why Desktop Commander? Unlike regular AI chatbots, Desktop Commander can actually find and move files on your computer — organizing your invoices for real, not just telling you how.
